The Vedic Protocol

How We Craft Arogya Nidhi Bilona Ghee

Most companies melt raw commercial butter using steam boilers to make ghee in 30 minutes. That is not ghee; it is milk fat. We stick strictly to the traditional 5-step Vedic system.

1

Fresh Milking

Milking grass-fed pure bred cows in natural environments.

2

Setting into Whole Curd

Raw milk is boiled and naturally cooled to culture and set overnight into yogurt.

3

Bilona Churning

Using bi-directional wooden churns, yogurt is whipped continuously to separate buttermilk from premium butter (Makkhan).

4

Clay Pot Slow Melting

Butter is slow heated over eco-friendly wooden fuel fires till the liquids evaporate and only golden crystals remain.
